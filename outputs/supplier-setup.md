## Senior Cat Kidney Support Powder Dropshipping Guide

## 1. Registering on CJ Dropshipping

### Step 1.1: Create Your Account

1. Navigate to **https://cjdropshipping.com**
2. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top-right corner
3. Choose your account type: **Dropshipper** (not manufacturer)
4. Enter the following details:
   - Email address (use your business email for credibility)
   - Password (minimum 8 characters, include uppercase, number, and symbol)
   - Business name: **PawVital SA**
   - Country: **South Africa**
   - Phone number (include +27 country code, e.g., +27 82 123 4567)

5. Check the terms of service and privacy policy
6. Click **"Create Account"**

### Step 1.2: Verify Your Email

1. Check your email inbox for the CJ Dropshipping verification link
2. Click the verification link within 24 hours
3. Return to CJ Dropshipping and log in with your credentials

### Step 1.3: Complete Your Business Profile

1. Click your **profile icon** (top-right) → **"Account Settings"**
2. Fill in the following sections:

   **Business Information:**
   - Company name: PawVital SA (Pty) Ltd
   - Registration number: (if applicable)
   - Business type: Online Retail / E-commerce
   - Industry: Pet Health & Wellness

   **Contact Information:**
   - Full name (owner or manager)
   - Business address (must be valid South African physical address)
   - City, Province, Postal Code
   - Phone and email

   **Bank/Payment Information** (see Step 1.4)

3. Upload a company logo (optional but recommended for professional appearance)
4. Write a brief business description (2-3 sentences about PawVital SA)

### Step 1.4: Add a Payment Method

CJ Dropshipping accepts multiple payment methods:

**Recommended for South Africa:**

- **PayPal** (most common for SA dropshippers)
  - Go to **Account Settings** → **Payment Methods**
  - Click **"Add PayPal"**
  - Log in with your PayPal account and authorize CJ Dropshipping
  - Ensure your PayPal account is verified with a valid ZAR or USD currency option

- **Credit/Debit Card** (Visa, Mastercard)
  - Click **"Add Card"**
  - Enter card details (most cards work; international transaction fees may apply)
  - Add billing address (must match your business address)

- **Stripe** (alternative for USD payments)
  - Available for businesses with verified Stripe accounts

**⚠️ Warning:** Avoid cryptocurrency payment options if you're new; stick with PayPal or cards for tracking and dispute resolution.

### Step 1.5: Verify Your Account (Optional but Recommended)

1. Click **"Account Settings"** → **"Verification"**
2. Choose **"Business Verification"**
3. Upload:
   - Business registration document (CIPC B-BBEE certificate or similar)
   - ID copy (front and back)
   - Proof of address (utility bill, lease agreement, dated within 3 months)

**Timeline:** Verification takes 1-3 business days. This unlocks higher order limits and priority support.

## 2. Finding the Senior Cat Kidney Support Powder

### Step 2.1: Search for the Product

1. Log in to CJ Dropshipping
2. Click **"Products"** in the main menu
3. In the search bar, type one of the following:
   - "Senior Cat Kidney Support Powder"
   - "Cat Kidney Support Formula"
   - "Senior Cat Supplement Powder"
   - "Feline Kidney Health Powder"

### Step 2.2: Apply Filters to Narrow Results

1. **Category:** Pet Supplies → Pet Health & Supplements
2. **Price Range:** Set between 8-25 USD (wholesale cost)
3. **Minimum Order Quantity:** Prefer 1 or small quantities for testing
4. **Shipping:** Select "Ships to South Africa" or check individual listings
5. **Supplier Rating:** Filter for suppliers with 4.8+ stars and 100+ reviews

### Step 2.3: Evaluate Product Listings — Quality Checklist

For each product listing, check:

**Product Details:**
- ✅ Clear product images (minimum 5-8 photos showing packaging, contents, labels)
- ✅ Detailed description with ingredients list visible
- ✅ Weight/size specification (e.g., 100g, 200g powder)
- ✅ Nutritional information or ingredient breakdown
- ✅ Target audience explicitly states "Senior Cats" or "Kidney Health"

**Compliance & Safety:**
- ✅ Product is **NOT** labeled as "homeopathic" only (requires proper SADC/NRCS registration in SA)
- ✅ No claims of curing disease (supplements can only "support" or "promote")
- ✅ Packaging shows proper labeling in English (required for SA import)
- ✅ Safety certifications mentioned (ISO, GMP, or similar)

**Supplier Information:**
- ✅ Supplier name and history visible
- ✅ Minimum 4.8-star rating
- ✅ 500+ positive reviews
- ✅ Response time listed as "within 24 hours" or less
- ✅ No major complaints about quality or shipping damage

**Pricing:**
- ✅ Wholesale cost between 8-25 USD per unit
- ✅ Transparent shipping cost to South Africa
- ✅ No hidden platform fees

## 3. Requesting Product Samples

### Step 3.1: Why Request Samples?

Before committing to bulk dropshipping, sampling is **essential**:
- Verify actual product quality vs. listing photos
- Test packaging for shipping damage durability (critical for 15-day SA transit)
- Check ingredient accuracy and expiration dates
- Confirm nutritional claims and customer communication points
- Assess if product justifies your planned retail markup
